Subdivide is a game about reading rhythms. You need a basic understanding of music notation to play.

Each level will show you a rhythm, and will give you a full bar's count-in after a brief "warm up" period.

Complete levels by accurately tapping their rhythm on the space bar, or on the screen.

To show the first level, tap!
